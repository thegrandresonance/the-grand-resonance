# Nexus | Unified Business Intelligence

> **Stop Managing Chaos. Start Orchestrating Growth.**
> Nexus is an open architectural blueprint for a unified dashboard that brings all your online businesses, analytics, and tasks into a single, AI-powered command center.

Entrepreneurs and solopreneurs are drowning in tabs: Google Analytics, Shopify, WordPress, ad platforms, support tickets. Context switching is killing productivity.

## Core Philosophy & Principles

1.  **Unification, Not Replacement:** Plug into your existing stack. We build connectors, not competitors.
2.  **AI-Native, Not AI-Bolted:** Intelligence is the core, not a feature. Proactive alerts, automated summaries, and predictive insights.
3.  **Privacy-First:** Your data belongs to you. Nexus can be self-hosted. We don't want your data; we want to help you understand it.
4.  **Composable Architecture:** Pick the modules you need. E-commerce analytics? Content performance? Project management? Enable what matters to you.
